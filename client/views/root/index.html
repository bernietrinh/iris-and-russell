Iris' Story

I walked into math class and I saw my friend. I wanted to sit with her despite this Hawaiian shirt guy comfortably sitting there. I asked and waited patiently. He got up and went to a different spot. I couldn’t help but be moved by his kindness. I gratefully smiled at him as I took off my jacket.

Russell's Story

I walked into math class, sporting my Hawaiian attire with my hair sleekly parted in half, thinking I was the sh*t. I sat down in the first seat I saw. Then this small girl had the nerve to ask me to move so she can sit with her friend. I gave her my ‘are you for real look’. The tiny girl continued to stand there and stared at me. I unwillingly got up and gave her my seat. I sat in my new spot, salty…until she unzipped her neon yellow bubble jacket and smirked at me. And my day just got better...

With that, they started dating after high school. Days turned to weeks, then weeks to years. There were tears, laughter, disagreements, gas holding, but their infinite love for each other made them best friends. And here we are today, celebrating 12 years together with many more to come.
